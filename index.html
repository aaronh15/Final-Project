喜羊羊與灰太狼

xi yang yang yu hui tailang

喜羊羊; Xǐ Yángyáng; 'Happy Sheep'

Weslie, a school-aged goat, is a positive, intelligent, and supportive archetypal hero. He takes leadership roles, detects danger, and forgives Wolffy. He enjoys playing pranks and becoming friends with Tibbie.

慢羊羊; Màn Yángyáng; 'Slow Sheep'

Slowy, a slow and steady goat, serves as the village elder and schoolmaster, teaching students valuable lessons through trickery and experiments. Inspired by Albert Einstein, he has wild hair and spectacles.

懒羊羊; Lǎn Yángyáng; 'Lazy Sheep'

A small, gluttonous goat wearing a yellow drool bib. He hates working and likes to sleep, but due to his lazy nature, he is often the first caught by Wolffy.

美羊羊; Měi Yángyáng; 'Beautiful Sheep'

A female goat in Green Green Grassland, who always wears pink bows on her horns and pink shoes and a pink scarf with a white line. She is one of Slowy's students and is generally liked by all the goats in Green Green Grassland.

沸羊羊; Fèi Yángyáng; 'Boiling Sheep'

Sparky is the only dark-skinned goat. He is a school-aged goat that attends the same school as Weslie. Sparky is depicted as a jock character.

暖羊羊; Nuǎn Yángyáng; 'Warm Sheep'

She is a docile and kind female goat who is class president of Weslie's class in school. She is revealed in the Olympics special to possess mountain goat genes, effectively making her the strongest goat in Goat Village above Sparky.

灰太狼; Huī Tàiláng; 'Grey Wolf'

Wolffy is the main antagonist of the series. Wolffy is the 250th generation grandson of Wu Dalang, the ancestor of the wolf clan, and the king of the wolves of the Green Green Grassland.

红太狼; Hóng Tàiláng; 'Red Wolf'

The narcissistic wife of Wolffy, who dresses in a red robe with black and white trims. She wears red lipstick, make-up, and two hoops on her feet.

小灰灰; Xiǎo Huīhuī; 'Little Grey'

He is the son of Wolffy and Wolnie. He is a cute pup with big eyes. He is approximately 2–6 years old in human years. He is a crybaby, but is very innocent.
